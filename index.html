  • Recursive Factorial

  • The factorial of a any non-negative integer is denoted by n! It is defined to be the product of all integers from 1 to n, i.e n! = n * (n-1) * (n-2) *... * 1
  • An interesting property of factorials is that n! = n * (n-1)! This property can be exploited to compute the factorial of an integer in a recursive manner.
